Conclusion and Future Growth Plans

Summary of key takeaways from our business model

VendoPharmacy modernises medication access by combining 24/7 automated dispensing, secure pharmacist-controlled POM lockers, and real-time NUH telepharmacy counselling. Supported by Singpass + EPS verification, a robust quality assurance framework, and VendAI decision support, our model delivers medication access that is convenient, compliant, and patient-centred.

This is made possible through strategic partnerships with NUH, which safeguards clinical decision-making through pharmacist oversight, and Hyphens Pharma, which ensures a reliable and consistent supply chain. Together, these collaborations enable VendoPharmacy to reduce waiting times, ease hospital pharmacy load, and strengthen continuity of care for patients.
