Vision

To modernise pharmacy access in Singapore by becoming a trusted, technology-enabled extension of healthcare institutions, providing seamless, patient-centred medication access anytime and anywhere.

Multi-Tier Medication Access via Smart Vending

Vendo Pharmacy offers General Sales List (GSL) and Pharmacy-Only (P) medications directly through vending machines, with Prescription-Only Medicines (POM) securely dispensed via pharmacist-controlled lockers attached to the machines.

This ensures regulatory compliance while expanding medication accessibility, allowing patients to access different categories of medications based on their needs and prescription requirements.

Key Benefits:

  • Immediate access to GSL and P medications
  • Secure POM collection via verified locker system
  • Full regulatory compliance with HSA guidelines
  • Convenient 24/7 availability

Decentralised Medication Collection System

By enabling medication collection outside traditional hospital pharmacies, Vendo Pharmacy significantly reduces waiting times, alleviates hospital pharmacy congestion, and improves convenience for patients who require repeat or chronic medications.

Patients can collect their prescribed medications at convenient locations near MRT stations and residential estates, eliminating the need to travel to hospitals during busy hours.

Advantages:

  • Reduced waiting times for medication collection
  • Decreased congestion at hospital pharmacies
  • Convenient collection points in high-traffic areas
  • Ideal for working adults and caregivers
  • No appointment or queue required

Customised Medication Kits for Specific Needs

Vendo Pharmacy provides tailored travel medication kits adjusted according to travel duration and destination. This personalized approach reduces medication wastage, lowers patient costs, and ensures appropriate medication supply.

Each kit is customized based on the traveler's destination, duration of travel, and specific health needs, ensuring patients only purchase what they need.

Features:

  • Duration-based medication quantities
  • Destination-specific health recommendations
  • Reduced medication wastage
  • Cost-effective solutions
  • Pharmacist-verified kit contents

24/7 Tele-Pharmacy Counselling

All purchases of P and POM medications are supported by round-the-clock pharmacist tele-counselling, ensuring patients receive proper usage instructions, safety advice, and clinical guidance equivalent to in-person pharmacy consultations.

Our licensed pharmacists are available 24/7 via video consultation to answer questions, provide medication counselling, and ensure safe and appropriate medication use.

Counselling Includes:

  • Proper medication usage instructions
  • Potential side effects and warnings
  • Drug interaction checks
  • Dosage and timing guidance
  • Storage recommendations
  • Follow-up advice

VendAI: AI-Assisted Decision Support System

VendAI is an AI-assisted recommendation and decision-support system that provides personalised suggestions based on patient inputs, usage patterns, and clinical guidelines. VendAI supports pharmacists and enhances medication appropriateness and patient confidence, without replacing professional judgement.

The system analyzes patient symptoms, medical history, and current medications to provide tailored recommendations, while flagging potential concerns that require pharmacist consultation.

VendAI Capabilities:

  • Personalized medication recommendations
  • Symptom-based product suggestions
  • Drug interaction warnings
  • Clinical guideline compliance
  • Usage pattern analysis
  • Pharmacist escalation when needed

Important: VendAI is a support tool and does not replace professional pharmacist judgement. All P and POM medications require pharmacist consultation.
