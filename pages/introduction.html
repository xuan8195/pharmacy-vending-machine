About Vendo Pharmacy

An automated pharmacy vending solution providing round-the-clock access to essential medicines, supported by tele-pharmacy counselling and secure identity verification

What is Vendo Pharmacy?

Vendo Pharmacy is an automated pharmacy vending solution that provides 24/7 access to medicines, supported by tele-pharmacy counselling and secure identity verification.

Vendo Pharmacy is led by the pharmacy owner and supported by an in-house pharmacist who oversees governance, smart vending, lockers, and case escalation. A support team ensures compliance and process improvements, while delivery staff handle secure stock replenishment.

To enhance continuity of care and patient trust, we partner with National University Hospital (NUH) and Hyphens Pharma to strengthen healthcare linkage and inventory support, ensuring safe, timely and reliable patient access to a dependable medication supply ecosystem.

COLLABORATIONS

Key Partnerships

Strategic collaborations ensuring quality, safety, and reliability in medication access

National University Hospital (NUH)

National University Hospital (NUH) supports VendoPharmacy by providing extensive pharmacist support to enable a safe and clinically governed pathway for Pharmacy-only (P) and prescription-only medications (POM) through counselling. This strengthens patient confidence that VendoPharmacy prioritises clinical standards, not just convenience.

Hyphens Pharma

Hyphens Pharma supports VendoPharmacy by ensuring a reliable and safe medication supply across our machines. This helps minimise stock-outs, reduce wastage, and maintain medication quality, so users can trust that what they receive is legitimate and properly managed.

How It Works

Purchasing a medication: Simple, secure, and convenient medication access in 4 easy steps

1

Locate & Browse

Find a nearby VendoPharmacy unit and browse available products on-screen. Tap to view details such as price, quantity, directions, and key warnings.

2

Get Smart Guidance (VendAI)

Unsure which medication is suitable? Answer a few quick questions and VendAI will guide you with personalised recommendations.

3

Pharmacist Review

For Pharmacy-only medicines* or if safety checks are triggered, a licensed pharmacist will review and authorise the purchase before dispensing.

4

Pay & collect

Complete payment and collect your medication. A dispensing record will be stored

Accessing a Secure Locker: Simple, secure, and convenient medication access in 4 easy steps

1

Obtain Prescription

Consult doctor from supporting partners and obtain a prescription. Select on Healthhub app preference for Vendopharmacy collection and state desired location.

2

Verify Identity

Use Singpass to verify your identity and access prescription medications securely. Caregivers may use the caregiver function in the healthhub app to collect medications on behalf of individuals.

3

Pharmacist consultation

Pharmacist will perform adequate track and compulsory tele consultation to ensure safe medication use

4

Pay & collect

Complete payment and collect your medication. A dispensing record will be stored

Revenue Model

Medication Sales

  • • 24/7 sales of General Sale products including high-turnover OTC items and travel medication kits
  • • 24/7 Pharmacy-only product after Singpass verification and real-time tele-counselling.
  • • 24/7 Prescription-only locker collection after Singpass verification, valid e-prescription from NUH and real-time tele-counselling.

Tele-Pharmacy & Consultation Fees

  • • Patients purchasing P or POM items are charged a small professional fee for real-time video counselling with registered pharmacists
  • • Higher consultation fees can be applied during peak periods or extended hours, reflecting the value of reduced waiting time and accessibility

Commission-Based Partnership Model

  • • Medications are supplied directly from Hyphens, with profits shared via a commission-based model, reducing procurement costs while scaling sales volume efficiently
  • • Acts as an extended dispensing arm for NUH, supporting patient medication collection, generating service and dispensing revenue

Value-Added Bundles & Upselling

  • • AI-supported inventory analysis ensures fast-moving, high-margin items are prioritized, improving overall revenue per machine
  • • By recommending only clinically appropriate add-ons (with a clear “why”), patients are more likely to buy a complete self-care set in one transaction (e.g., ORS + antidiarrhoeal advice, thermometer + fever meds), which increases average basket size while staying ethical

AI-Driven Cost Efficiency & Wastage Reduction

  • • AI-based demand forecasting optimises replenishment cycles and stock allocation, reducing overstocking and emergency restocking costs
  • • Smarter inventory rotation (e.g., FEFO) minimises medication expiry and wastage, protecting margins and improving overall profitability per machine

Higher Profitability via Lower Operating Cost (Margin Expansion)

  • • Centralised pharmacist coverage + standardised workflows lets one team support many machines, reducing per-site manpower cost while maintaining professional oversight
  • • Remote monitoring + forecast-driven replenishment reduces downtime, emergency restocking, and losses from expiry, improving gross margin per machine (same sales, lower cost base)

Expanding Our Services

More than just medication collection.

  • In the future, we plan to introduce simple community health support features such as blood pressure monitoring kiosks, basic wellness checks, and seasonal health campaigns. These services are designed to help you stay on top of your health in a convenient and accessible way.
  • We're also working with healthcare institutions to make repeat prescription collection even smoother, helping patients stay consistent with their medications and avoid missed refills.
  • For travellers, we aim to expand our travel health offerings with more destination-specific kits and easy-to-understand health guidance — so you can travel prepared and with peace of mind.

Smarter, Safer Technology

Technology that supports — not replaces — care.

  • We're continuously improving VendAI to provide clearer guidance when selecting medications. This means better prompts, clearer safety alerts, and stronger encouragement to speak to a pharmacist when needed.
  • Behind the scenes, we're strengthening our systems to reduce downtime, so machines remain reliable and available when you need them.
  • We're also enhancing secure data handling and prescription traceability to ensure your information remains protected and responsibly managed at all times.

Growing Our Network

Bringing convenience closer to you.

  • We plan to work with more polyclinics, GP clinics, and hospitals to make prescription collection more accessible across Singapore. This means more locations where you can collect your medications easily and securely.
  • At the same time, we're strengthening partnerships with trusted suppliers to maintain stable medication supply and fair pricing.
  • As we expand, we'll roll out machines strategically in high-demand areas such as residential estates, transport hubs, and near hospitals — so help is always within reach.

Sustainable & Responsible Growth

Caring for your health — and the environment.

  • We are committed to reducing medication wastage by improving demand forecasting and ensuring medicines are dispensed within safe usage timelines. Where needed, we work with partners to safely dispose of unsuitable or near-expiry stock in a compliant manner.
  • Our expansion will also focus on clustered deployment and optimised refill routes to reduce unnecessary transport. Combined with energy-efficient machines, this helps lower our overall environmental footprint while keeping services reliable.

Target

As we grow, our focus remains on making healthcare more accessible, reliable, and sustainable for everyone. We plan to introduce community health support features and expand our travel health bundles, while working closely with partners to reduce missed prescription pickups by at least 25%. At the same time, we are strengthening safety checks and secure prescription handling to maintain over 95% machine reliability, so you can depend on us whenever you need care. We are also committed to reducing medication wastage and improving energy efficiency — caring for both your health and the environment.
