Pirate Funnel (AARRR)

Our customer acquisition and retention strategy through Awareness, Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Revenue, and Referral

The AARRR Framework

A comprehensive approach to customer lifecycle management, from discovery to advocacy

1. Awareness
Reaching potential customers
2. Acquisition
First-time user onboarding
3. Activation
First positive experience
4. Retention
Repeat usage and loyalty
5. Revenue
Monetization streams
6. Referral
Word-of-mouth growth

1. Awareness

Making Vendo Pharmacy visible to our target audience

High-traffic placement

Deploy machines at MRT stations, hospitals, polyclinics-adjacent areas, and residential estates to maximise exposure.

Clear branding + value proposition

Prominent signage (e.g., "24/7 access to medication", "Pharmacist support via tele-consult") to communicate safety and convenience quickly.

Educational messaging

Through launch-phase ambassadors, public education campaign and social media mass outreach to help reduce public unfamiliarity with vending-based pharmacy services and builds initial trust in the model.

Institutional credibility

Collaboration with NUH/healthcare partners where staff inform suitable patients about vending-based collection options.

2. Acquisition

Converting awareness into first-time users

Low-barrier onboarding

First-time users are acquired through a low-barrier, intuitive step-by-step touchscreen journey that guides medication selection and verification reduces hesitation and complexity.

Trust through compliance

Singpass verification for P and POM medicines reassures users that identity checks and safety standards are upheld.

Multiple payment

QR code, cashless payment, cash remove financial friction and encourage trial use.

Entry products to encourage trial

Promote popular, low-risk items and travel medication kits as easy first purchases.

Convenience positioning

Emphasise "no queues / no appointment" for working adults, caregivers, and after-hours needs.

3. Activation

Delivering a positive first experience that creates value

Rapid Fulfillment Speed

Designed to complete a transaction under 60 seconds, immediately demonstrating the time-saving advantage over traditional retail pharmacies.

Seamless Singpass Integration

Singpass login enables secure identity confirmation and supports digital prescription checks (via EPS where applicable), reducing manual steps and friction.

On-Demand Pharmacist Consultation

One-tap Live Tele-consult provides real-time reassurance for P/POM decisions and ensures pharmacist-authorised dispensing.

High reliability at the collection point

Dispensing confirmation (e.g., drop detection sensors) reduces failed transactions and builds trust that the system works as intended.

Clear guidance for confident decisions

VendAI guided Q&A helps users choose suitable low-risk products, flags red-flag symptoms, and escalates to pharmacist review when necessary.

4. Retention

Building long-term engagement and repeat usage

Always-available convenience

24/7 access drives long-term reliance encourages long-term reliance on the service due to high convenience.

Reliability + stock confidence

AI-driven inventory reduce frustration with strong inventory and uptime management.

Consistent safety standards

Maintain trust via compliance, audit readiness, and pharmacist oversight.

Increase reliability

Through remote monitoring, proactive servicing, fast incident resolution, ensure that our vending machine is running smoothly and safely.

Marketing incentives

Marketing incentives are deployed to encourage repeat usage and embed the vending pharmacy into patients' long-term care journeys.

5. Revenue

Diversified monetization streams

Product Sale

Revenue is generated through the sale of GSL, pharmacy-only (P), and eligible prescription-only (POM) medicines.

Consultation Fee

Consultation with pharmacy for dispense of P or POM medication.

Value Added Bundle

Bundled products such as travel medication kits increase average transaction value.

Partnership model

Partnership with Hyphens Pharma ensures a stable supply chain and enables commission-based revenue sharing.

Automation

Reduces manpower costs and improves inventory turnover, enhancing financial sustainability.

Reduced wastage

Reduction medication wastage and expiry further supports long-term profitability.

6. Referral

Turning satisfied users into advocates

Word of mouth

Caregivers recommend the service to family members managing regular medication needs. Working adults share the service as a time-saving alternative to physical pharmacies.

Partner enabled referrals

Healthcare professionals contribute to referrals by informing and educating patients about vending-based collection options.

User Trust

Accurate dispensing, convenience to retrieve medication and pharmacist counselling strengthens word-of-mouth credibility.
