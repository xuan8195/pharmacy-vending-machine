Problems We Solve

Long waiting times

  • Vendo Pharmacy reduces waiting time and improves accessibility to medications through a secure locker–vending system.

Limited access to medications outside of office hours

  • The 24/7 vending pharmacy located in residential areas provides round-the-clock access to essential medications, allowing patients to access essential medications anytime without having to worry about when pharmacies open and close.

Inefficient access to single medications

  • Located within residential areas, hence allows residents to quickly and efficiently obtain medications nearby without unnecessary travel.

Hospital pharmacy congestion

  • Offloads repeat-medication collection to pharmacist-controlled lockers, easing peak-hour crowding and improving hospital pharmacy workflow.

Manpower constraints in healthcare

  • Automates routine transactions and triage support, allowing pharmacists to focus on higher-value clinical care and complex cases.

Disclaimer & User Safety

Guidelines and safeguards to ensure safe and responsible medication access

1

No diagnosis

VendAI provides guideline-based recommendations for low-risk scenarios. It does not diagnose, and it does not replace a doctors' assessments.

2

Red-flag escalation

If symptoms suggest a potentially serious condition, VendAI will direct users to urgent care / to a real-time pharmacist consult.

3

Privacy & consent

Singpass verification and EPS retrieval occur only with user consent. Data access is purpose-limited, logged for audit, and used to support safe dispensing and counselling.

4

Not for Emergency

VendAI is not for emergencies. If severe symptoms occur, users should call emergency services immediately.

Revenue Model

Medication Sales

  • • 24/7 sales of General Sale products including high-turnover OTC items and travel medication kits
  • • 24/7 Pharmacy-only product after Singpass verification and real-time tele-counselling.
  • • 24/7 Prescription-only locker collection after Singpass verification, valid e-prescription from NUH and real-time tele-counselling.

Tele-Pharmacy & Consultation Fees

  • • Patients purchasing P or POM items are charged a small professional fee for real-time video counselling with registered pharmacists
  • • Higher consultation fees can be applied during peak periods or extended hours, reflecting the value of reduced waiting time and accessibility

Commission-Based Partnership Model

  • • Medications are supplied directly from Hyphens, with profits shared via a commission-based model, reducing procurement costs while scaling sales volume efficiently
  • • Acts as an extended dispensing arm for NUH, supporting patient medication collection, generating service and dispensing revenue

Value-Added Bundles & Upselling

  • • AI-supported inventory analysis ensures fast-moving, high-margin items are prioritized, improving overall revenue per machine
  • • VendAI Assistant drives revenue by enabling 24/7 self-service GSL sales and escalating suitable cases to telepharmacy consultations that generate higher-value medication sales and consultation fees

AI-Driven Cost Efficiency & Wastage Reduction

  • • AI-based demand forecasting optimises replenishment cycles and stock allocation, reducing overstocking and emergency restocking costs
  • • Smarter inventory rotation (e.g., FEFO) minimises medication expiry and wastage, protecting margins and improving overall profitability per machine

VendAI Assistant

VendAI Assistant initiates with a low-risk, pre-identification symptom triage to guide users and detect red-flag conditions. For cases that do not require professional consultation or restricted medicines, VendAI provides guideline-based, personalised recommendations directly to the user. It also displays and verbalises counselling points for GSL items.

When a personalised assessment or telepharmacy consultation is needed, users undergo Singpass-based identity verification, enabling secure retrieval of medication history via EPS and access to pharmacist guidance for a consultation fee.

24/7 Telepharmacy

The telepharmacy service operates continuously, enabling round-the-clock purchase of GSL and P medications and collection of POM medications from secure lockers adjacent to the vending machine. For all P and POM items, real-time pharmacist counselling is conducted via an on-screen interface to verify suitability and authorise dispensing. This ensures patients can access regulated medications safely at any time without being constrained by pharmacy operating hours.

Travel Medication Kits

Our travel medication kits are tailored to each traveller's destination and trip duration, providing only what is necessary for safe and convenient travel. This reduces the need to purchase multiple medications from different sources, minimises medication wastage, lowers overall costs, and ensures an appropriate and sufficient supply throughout the journey.

POM Lockers

By enabling medication collection outside traditional hospital pharmacies, Vendo Pharmacy reduces waiting times, alleviates hospital pharmacy congestion, and improves convenience for patients who require repeat or chronic medications.

Quality Control

Our pharmacy vending machines are governed by a comprehensive quality assurance framework to ensure medication safety, reliability, and regulatory compliance. This includes:

  • Regular internal audits
  • Strict First Expiry First Out (FEFO) and expiry monitoring
  • Immediate removal of expired or unsafe stock
  • Clear SOPs and full documentation
  • System traceability for accountability
  • Early detection of issues
  • Continuous quality improvement processes

These measures ensure patients receive safe and appropriate medications at all times.

AI for Stock Level Monitoring

AI monitoring is used to track sales trends (e.g. during peak seasons), refill frequency (average refill cycle of 2 days/week), and past stockouts. The system automatically adjusts reorder levels and supports proactive refilling before stock reaches critical levels (20-30% remaining stock), ensuring smooth stock replenishment and minimizing downtime.

AI for Expiry Date Management

AI is used to conduct expiry checks and support FEFO (First Expired, First Out) stock rotation through daily automated tracking. Near-expiry medications are flagged 3 months in advance for removal or replenishment, reducing medication wastage by ~30–50%, keeping expired-stock incidents to less than 1% annually, and supporting ≥98% compliance with expiry control standards to maintain medication safety and quality.

Tele-pharmacy Services

Tele pharmacy enables real-time pharmacist counselling through an on-screen system with a camera and microphone, allowing patients to ask questions and fulfil counselling requirements for P and POM medications. The service targets a pharmacist response time of less than 2 minutes, averages 3–5 minutes per consult, and supports 100% counselling documentation for auditability and patient safety.

Online Prescription Services

The system integrates with the Electronic Prescription System (EPS) to retrieve prescriptions digitally after Singpass verification, enabling eligible patients to collect POM medications via secured lockers without returning to hospital pharmacies. Authentication can be completed in under 30 seconds, reducing unnecessary pharmacy return visits and easing hospital pharmacy patient load by an estimated ~15 – 25%.

Maintaining medication quality

Machines are equipped with automated temperature and humidity controls, secure access, and routine monitoring to ensure medication quality and comply with HSA regulatory guidelines. Environmental conditions are monitored 24 hours a day, targeting storage at 20–25°C, with alerts triggered if deviations exceed ± 2°C.

AI assisted recommendation system (GSL & P items)

Use of AI in a structured questionnaire of 5–10 symptom questions to assess patients’ symptoms and recommend appropriate products/medications. Red-flag responses trigger automatic escalation to a pharmacist, reducing inappropriate GSL and P item selection by ~40%, and ensuring 100% pharmacist override for safety.

Interoperability + Secure Data Exchange

Vendo Pharmacy integrates with EPS/health IT systems for digital prescription retrieval, while a tamper-proof log records prescription access and locker collection to strengthen security, traceability, and PDPA-aligned accountability. This creates an end-to-end security chain where every access is authenticated, logged, and attributable via a 100% traceable audit trail, supporting audit readiness and preventing unauthorised access.
