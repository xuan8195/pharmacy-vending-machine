Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To improve access to medicines by delivering convenient, safe, and pharmacist-supported services through automated vending and tele-pharmacy solutions, without compromising regulatory compliance or patient care.

Our Vision

To modernise pharmacy access in Singapore by becoming a trusted, technology-enabled extension of healthcare institutions, providing seamless, patient-centred medication access anytime and anywhere.

VendoPharmacy integrates 24/7 automated medication access, tele-pharmacy services, and AI-assisted recommendations into a single platform. We ensure professional oversight, HSA regulatory compliance, and personalised care at every step.
